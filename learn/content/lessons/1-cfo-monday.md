## Concept

A **Finance Command Center** is a single place where a CFO sees cash health, spending, collections, payables, and vendor risk — updated continuously, not just at month-end close.

Traditional finance teams produce reports weekly or monthly. By then, a $77K conference overage or a duplicate invoice may already be paid. This platform closes the gap between **data** and **decision**.

## Acme example

Acme Robotics is a Series B robotics company:
- **$18M raised** (March 2025, Bessemer)
- **47 employees**, SLO-based compensation
- **$12.68M cash** ($11.18M operating + $1.5M reserve)
- **~$1.34M/month burn** — 34–58% above the $850K–$1.0M guidance
- **~9.5 months runway** at current burn (target: 18+ months)

Key live issues in the demo data:
- MODEX ($45K) + ProMat ($32K) conference budget overages
- Apex Logistics duplicate invoices ($14.5K)
- MidWest Fulfillment $48K AR, 92 days overdue

## So what?

A real CFO's Monday morning looks like:
1. **Glance at vitals** — cash, runway, AR overdue, AP at risk (30 seconds)
2. **Read the executive summary** — what's on fire, ranked by dollar impact
3. **Decide** — approve, delegate, or dismiss each flagged item
4. **Drill down** — only where needed, into the specialist agent tab

The platform doesn't replace judgment. It **prioritizes** what needs judgment first.

## On the dashboard

| Area | What to look at |
|------|----------------|
| **Top nav** | 🏦 Briefing → Treasury → Forecast → Budget → Collections → Payables → Data |
| **KPI Bar** | Four metric cards just below the nav — your vital signs |
| **↻ Update** | Top right — runs all 7 agents (~60 seconds) |
| **Action Items sidebar** | Right side — open decisions that need your input |
| **⚗ Hypo button** | Top right — switch to Scenario mode for safe simulation |

See the [Screen Guide](/screens) entries for **KPI Bar** and **CFO Briefing**.

## Try it

1. Open the dashboard at [acme-finance-agents.vercel.app](https://acme-finance-agents.vercel.app)
2. Click **↻ Update** (top right) and watch the button count progress (e.g., `◌ 3/7`)
3. On CFO Briefing, read the **Executive Pulse** section
4. Count how many RED vs YELLOW vs GREEN findings appear
5. Click one agent name in the "Agents Handled Automatically" section to jump to that tab

"Finance teams drown in spreadsheets and month-end reports. This platform runs specialist AI analysts against live data every week, surfaces the highest-impact issues ranked by dollar amount, and turns RED findings into trackable action items — so the CFO spends Monday deciding, not searching."
